03 — Health & Fitness

An all-in-one, mobile-first progressive web app for training and nutrition: an interactive anatomical muscle map that recommends evidence-based exercises, a fast workout logger with PR detection, an autoregulated mesocycle planner with volume-landmark gauges, and calorie/macro tracking with food search.   • Workout logger with rest timer, PR/1RM detection, and progress charts

04 — Game Development

Mineblock — Browser Voxel Sandbox

A first-person Minecraft-style sandbox that runs entirely in the browser, built with Three.js and vanilla JavaScript — no game engine, no external art assets. Every block texture and item icon is painted procedurally on canvas at load time.

  • Infinite seeded terrain with caves, ore veins, and chunked streaming as you explore
  • Mining with per-tool break speeds and tiered harvest requirements
  • Full crafting progression: 2x2 inventory crafting, a 3x3 crafting table, and furnace smelting
  • Autosaves your world and inventory to the browser between visits

05 — Game Development

Vice Shores — Open-World Driving Sandbox

An original GTA-inspired sandbox built with Three.js — walk a 12x12 block city, steal or buy cars, drift through a 6-speed gearbox with RPM-based shifting, and shoot pistols/SMGs, all with procedural WebAudio sound and no external assets.

  • Drift physics with slip-angle steering, handbrake-induced oversteer, and tire smoke
  • 6-speed gearbox with RPM-based auto shifting and a live tachometer
  • Street races, courier jobs, and a gun range — all paying into a persistent in-game economy
  • Procedural engine, gunfire, and impact audio synthesized live with WebAudio
