01 Bio

I'm a fourth-year Bachelor of Commerce student at the Sprott School of Business at Carleton University, specializing in Accounting, and currently working as a Tax Analyst on Deloitte's Global Employer Services team — providing tax compliance and advisory services for high-net-worth clients. I'm pursuing my CPA designation through a master's program, with a long-term interest in personal finance, wealth management, and the technology shaping how both are delivered.

Outside of tax season, I'm a working photographer — shooting automotive, portrait, landscape, street, and astrophotography work since 2022 — and an enthusiastic early adopter of agentic AI tools, which I use to design and ship projects like this site. I bring the same attention to detail and curiosity to every project, whether it's a client return, a balance sheet, or a line of code.
